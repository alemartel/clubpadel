# Design Specification

## Color Palette

**Concept:** A combination of deep navy/oxygen blue and vibrant emerald green to convey technology, exclusivity, and efficiency.

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|--------|-----|--------|
| **Primary Dark** | Navy / Oxygen Blue | `#0F172A` | Section backgrounds (Hero/Footer), headings. |
| **Accent – Modern Green** | Emerald | `#10B981` | Main CTAs, benefit checkmarks, micro-interactions. |
| **Secondary – Slate Blue** | Slate | `#334155` | Secondary text and subtle borders. |
| **Page Background** | Light Slate | `#F1F5F9` | Overall background for a clean, airy feel. navbar |
| **Pure White** | White | `#FFFFFF` | Cards and clean reading sections. |

---

## Typography & Visual Style

**Goal:** Premium, modern, “expensive” tech look.

| Element | Font | Weight |
|---------|------|--------|
| **Headings** | Plus Jakarta Sans or Outfit | 700 |
| **Body text** | Inter | 400 |

---

## Borders & Shadows

- **Border radius:** 12–16px (soft corners, not sharp).
- **Shadows:** Very light and diffuse to create a floating effect.
  - Example: `0 10px 15px -3px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.1)`.

---

## Gradients

- **Main button gradient:** `#10B981` → `#059669`. Subtle depth, modern and clean.
